Our Services

We offer a wide range of waterproofing services to meet your needs.

Retrofit Waterproofing

Upgrade and enhance the waterproofing of existing structures with our retrofit waterproofing services. We provide solutions to improve the water resistance of older buildings, ensuring they meet current standards and remain protected from water damage.

Read More

Bitumen Waterproofing

Utilize bitumen-based waterproofing solutions for effective and durable protection against water infiltration. Our bitumen waterproofing services are ideal for roofs, foundations, and other critical areas, providing a reliable barrier against moisture.

Read More

Epoxy Grout Waterproofing

Apply epoxy grout for waterproofing and sealing joints and cracks in various surfaces. Epoxy grout is highly durable and resistant to water, chemicals, and stains, making it an excellent choice for areas exposed to moisture and harsh conditions.

Read More

PU Grout Injection

Use polyurethane (PU) grout injection for sealing and waterproofing cracks and joints in concrete structures. PU grout expands upon injection, effectively filling voids and stopping water leaks, providing a flexible and long-lasting waterproofing solution.

Read More

Slab Crack Repair

Address and repair cracks in concrete slabs to prevent water infiltration and further structural damage. Our slab crack repair services ensure the stability and longevity of your concrete surfaces.

Read More

Crawl Space Waterproofing

Our comprehensive crawl space waterproofing services will protect your crawl space from moisture and humidity. This helps prevent mold growth, wood rot, and structural damage.

Q1: What services does Mercury Water Proofing offer?

A1: Mercury Water Proofing provides services including basement waterproofing, foundation repair, crawl space encapsulation, and mold remediation. Our experts apply modern methods and materials to protect your property against water damage.

Q2: How do I get a quote for waterproofing services?

A2: You can get a quote by contacting us through our website or by calling our customer service number. We will schedule an inspection and provide you with a detailed estimate. Our quotes are comprehensive and transparent, with no hidden fees.

Q3: What areas do you serve?

A3: We serve the whole metropolitan area and the surrounding regions. Please contact us to confirm whether we service your location. Our team is able to handle projects in both the urban and rural settings.

Q4: How should I maintain my waterproofing system?

A4: The only way you can make your waterproofing system last longer is through regular maintenance. We recommend periodic inspections and prompt repairs. A5: Water stains, mold growth, musty odors, and dampness in your basement or crawl space are the common signs that may indicate the requirement of waterproofing services. These signs will call for the need to contact us for an inspection to avoid further damage.
