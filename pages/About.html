About the Company

Mercury Construction & Water Proofing Pvt. Ltd., established in 2074 B.S and located in Jhamsekhel, Lalitpur, Kathmandu, is a leading waterproofing contractor with extensive expertise in the field. Our comprehensive range of services covers all aspects of waterproofing work, utilizing various high-quality materials and modern techniques. What distinguishes us is our unwavering commitment to excellence, demonstrated through our proven expertise, superior workmanship, and reliable service. At Mercury Construction, our dedicated team takes pride in their competence and focus on customer satisfaction, with a strong reputation built on perfection, quality, and professional service delivery across countless successful projects.
